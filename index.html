Questions

Fair questions

Where do your price ranges come from?

Municipal permit filings near your zip code, published regional cost data, and quotes analyzed on Contrax. Every range in your report is labeled with its source and a confidence level — when the data is thin, we say so instead of guessing.

Is this legal advice?

No. Contrax gives you pricing information and negotiation help, not legal or professional advice. For contract disputes, talk to a lawyer.

Do contractors pay to be recommended?

No, and they never will. Contractors aren't our customer — you are. Verdicts are built from public records and document analysis, not sponsorships.

What if the quote is actually fair?

Then we tell you that, plainly — and you sign with confidence instead of doubt. "This is a fair price, here's why" is just as valuable as catching a bad one.

What trades do you cover?

Plumbing, HVAC, and roofing at launch — the highest-ticket, highest-risk jobs. More trades as our data earns the right to judge them.
