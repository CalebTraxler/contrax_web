Questions

Fair questions

Is the scan really free?

Yes — and during launch the full report is free too. Contrax is free for homeowners, period. We make money from businesses that pay for clearly labeled placement in the directory, never from you.

Where do your price ranges come from?

Published regional cost data, public permit records near your zip code, and quotes analyzed on Contrax. Every range is labeled with its source and a confidence level — when the data is thin, we say so instead of guessing.

Do contractors pay to look good on Contrax?

They can pay for one thing: a clearly labeled sponsored spot in the directory — like a billboard. They can never pay to change a verdict, a scan result, a fair-quote score, or their public record. Those come from public data and the documents themselves.

Is this legal advice?

No. Contrax gives you pricing information and negotiation help, not legal or professional advice. For contract disputes, talk to a lawyer.
